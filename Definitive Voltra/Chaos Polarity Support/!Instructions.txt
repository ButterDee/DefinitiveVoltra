-To install support for Chaos Polarity-

Copy and drop the included files (excluding this instruction text file!) to your installation of Chaos Polarity.



-To remove support for Chaos Polarity-

Delete "misc.hmm" and "mod.ini" from the installation of Chaos Polarity. Rename "misc_original.hmm.bak" to "misc.hmm", and
"mod_original.ini.bak" to "mod.ini".
